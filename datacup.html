Datacup is a data competition hosted by Desjardins and Desjardins Lab. The mandate was to create a survival booklet for the participants in the competition during the event of the Cooperathon 2018 which took place in Montreal. The participants were imaged to be pioneers in space and in order to survive the challenges they must face, the booklet was to guide them towards a successful mission. The overall design was approached with graphics in motion and clean and pure geometrical forms to reflect the abstract concept of space and light.
