Data Cup is an open-sourced data competition by Desjardins Lab. The mandate was to create a "survival" booklet for the participants of the data challenge proposed by Data Cup. The booklet was handed out during Cooperathon 2018.

The concept revolved around the participants being the next pioneers of space, with missions that explored different areas of the galaxy. The overall design reflects this concept through minimal shapes that represented motion, light and data visualization.
