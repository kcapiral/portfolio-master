saiko!
2017

Type
Website design and Development
Software
Dreamweaver, Photoshop, Illustrator

Saiko! Is a website showcasing the trendiest prefectures in Japan. The design approach is of the old and the new, quirky and interactive. As Japan is modern and also traditional, the website reflects this state with the choice of colors, typography and curated images. As a basic traveling guide, the user experiences the website as if they are traveling through one prefecture to another, as they move in directions towards a location, and into the location details.
