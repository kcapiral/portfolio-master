Matagora is a commercial space renting platform designed in collaboration with user experience and researcher, Vithu Namasivayam. As the user interface designer, my role involved the following: participation in the creation and reviewing of the sitemap, creating and establishing the brand identity, designing mockups, and making improvements based on changing needs.

Matagora was built in the framework, Angular, because the components were easy to implement and organize, and provided a fluid user experience. The user interface had to correspond to existing components from the library of Angular Material while presenting as a clean, crafty, and not-so-techy website. The design had to be minimal, to allow for a fluid and smooth experience of the website for scenarios of onboarding users to the platform, searching through space informations, and filling in forms.
