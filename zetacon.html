Zetacon is a luxurious home and commercial construction company. I was asked to redesign the website by improving the existing layout, typography, color, brand identity and curate the photography selections. The original design of the website was basic, unsuitable as a catalogue website, and didn’t reflect their overall identity.

The improved design reflects a sophisticated, timeless and minimal website with a rich color palette, a structured layout and a refined choice of typography. The user experience was also optimized for users to easily sift through the client’s images and details of projects through different screen sizes.
