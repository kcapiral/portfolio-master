A redesigned website that isn’t live, but fully designed as a mockup. The client asked to improve the existing layout, design and brand identity and curate and replace existing images. The original design of the website was basic, startup-ish and didn’t reflect their quality construction services and portfolio. The improved design brought in a sophisticated, timeless and minimal color palette, layout and typography. The user experience was also optimized for users to easily sift through the client’s images and details of projects through different screen sizes.
